- **Minea** to spy on winning products — €49/month

That's **€119/month** before you've spent a single euro on ads. And none of these tools understand dropshipping — they're built for generic marketers.

Huntly replaces all of them with one focused platform, starting at **€19/month**.

## What Huntly Does

Huntly gives dropshippers four AI-powered tools built specifically for the way they work. Every output is generated by Claude (Anthropic's AI — the most capable on the market) and trained to think like a dropshipper, not a copywriter.

---

### 1. Product Analyzer

Paste any product name, description, category and price point. Huntly tells you:

- **Go / Maybe / Avoid** verdict with reasoning
- **Saturation score** (1–10) — how crowded is the market right now
- **Virgin angle** — an untapped positioning no competitor is using
- **Central pain** — the real emotional trigger behind the purchase
- **Target person** — hyper-specific buyer profile, not just "women 18-35"
- **TikTok hook** — a ready-to-film opening line for your video
- **Main risk** — legal, logistical, or market risks before you invest
- **Best markets** — which countries to target first
- **Quick wins** — 3 actionable steps to launch profitably

This is the research phase that normally takes hours. Huntly does it in 20 seconds.

---

### 2. Copy Generator

Input your product and get 8 conversion-ready assets in one click:

- Headline + Subheadline
- Full product description
- Bullet points (benefit-focused, not feature lists)
- Meta ad primary text
- Meta ad headline
- Abandoned cart email subject line
- Abandoned cart email body

Choose between Conversational, Professional, or Urgent tone. Everything is copy-paste ready for your store, ads, and email flows.

---

### 3. Ad Script Creator

Choose your platform (TikTok, Instagram Reels, or both) and video length (15s, 30s, 60s). Huntly generates 3 complete video scripts, each with a different angle:

- **Hook** (first 0–3 seconds — the scroll-stopper)
- **Full script** with timing and delivery notes
- **Why it works** — the psychological principle behind each script

Three scripts per generation means you can split-test angles from day one instead of guessing which creative will win.

---

### 4. Competitor Spy

Paste any competitor's ad copy, product description, or store content. Huntly breaks down:

- What they're doing right (and why it works)
- Their **weak points** — gaps in their positioning you can exploit
- **Market gaps** they're missing entirely
- **Differentiation angles** — how to position your product against theirs
- A ready-to-use **headline** and **positioning statement** that beats them

This turns competitor research from a manual 2-hour job into a 30-second analysis.
